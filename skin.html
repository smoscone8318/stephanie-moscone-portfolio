Skin Sync Mobile App

OVERVIEW

SkinSync is a mobile skin care application that allows users to build their skincare routines, learn about taking care of their skin, purchase different products and give product recommendations based on skin type.

In a crowded skincare market, the absence of a personalized and accessible system for matching products to individual skin types leaves consumers navigating through overwhelming options, resulting in ineffective outcomes, potential skin irritation, and a pressing need for a comprehensive platform to provide tailored recommendations and planning tools.

ROLE

Product Designer

User Research, Interaction, Visual design, Prototyping & Testing

January 2024 - April 2024

GOALS

The goal of the app is to empower users to take control of their skin health by providing a platform to track their skin type and concerns, access personalized recommendations and products, and create customized skincare routines. By offering tailored advice and reminders, the app aims to help users achieve optimal skin health and maintain consistent skincare practices.

DISCOVERY

The qualitative research focused on individuals aged 18-40 to gain insights into their basic skincare practices and routines. By exploring these aspects, the study aimed to establish a better understanding of users' general skincare habits and their awareness of skin health. Through in-depth interviews or surveys, the research sought to uncover patterns, preferences, and knowledge gaps related to skincare, providing valuable insights for developing tailored skincare products or educational initiatives.

KEY INSIGHTS

Skincare users place high value on products that cater to their specific needs and offer clear purchase information. Many individuals adhere to skincare routines they have maintained for years, highlighting the importance of familiarity and consistency in their regimen. For skincare apps to be effective, they should provide personalized skincare routines, recommend suitable products, and offer reminders for consistent use.

Users also appreciate apps that offer educational content on ingredients, skin types, and common conditions, helping them make informed decisions. Pricing and suitability for specific skin types are crucial considerations for users when choosing skincare products. While some users may be hesitant about skincare apps, they are often intrigued by the prospect of receiving personalized tips and recommendations. Features such as step-by-step instructions and allergy information are particularly valued, contributing to a positive user experience.

RESULTS

The results of the app redesign include a simplified sign-in/sign-up process and improved navigation, enhancing the overall user experience. Users' suggestions for carousel scrolling for product display have been implemented, providing a more interactive and engaging way to view products. Additional category options based on product preferences have been added, offering users more choices and customization options. The frequency of the logo has been reduced to the home and profile pages, reducing visual clutter and improving the app's overall aesthetics. The logo is now excluded from action pages, further streamlining the user interface. A drop-down menu for linking facial routines has been introduced, providing clarity for users when linking routines to reminders. Overall, these changes have resulted in a more user-friendly and intuitive app that better meets the needs of users seeking personalized skincare solutions.
