Soft Peaks Website Design

OVERVIEW

Designing a website for Soft Peaks, a beloved Vancouver ice cream shop known for its premium local ingredients and delicious flavors, involves creating an engaging and user-friendly platform that captures the essence of the brand. Despite their large social media following, Soft Peaks lacks a central online hub where all their information is easily accessible.

The website will feature a modern and inviting design that highlights their commitment to quality and community. Key elements will include a Home Page, a Menu, Social Media links, and a Contact Page. By consolidating all relevant information into a cohesive and aesthetically pleasing website, Soft Peaks can enhance their online presence, attract new customers, and provide a seamless experience for their existing fanbase.

ROLE

Web Designer

User Research, Interaction, Visual design, Prototyping & Testing

July 2024 - Ongoing

GOALS

The key goals for designing the website for Soft Peaks are to achieve a balanced and visually appealing contrast between citrus, milky, and coffee tones, reflecting the diverse flavors offered by the shop. This can be achieved through a moodboard to compare and contrast color palettes to establish what will work best for the brand. The content will be concise, clearly advertising menu products and streamlining social media outreach to highlight the most relevant updates and promotions. Additionally, all website mockups will be fully optimized for mobile access, featuring responsive design elements for seamless browsing on different screen sizes, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable user experience for customers on the go. By focusing on these goals, the website will effectively represent Soft Peaks' unique offerings, enhance customer engagement, and provide a user-friendly platform accessible on all devices.

RESULTS

The design process for the Soft Peaks website was both challenging and rewarding. Through thorough research and discovery, we gained a deep understanding of the brand and its audience, allowing us to create a site that truly represents Soft Peaks' unique identity. The wireframing and prototyping phase ensured a user-friendly structure, and our visual design effectively captured the desired contrast between citrus, milky, and coffee tones. Concise content creation highlighted the quality and variety of Soft Peaks' offerings, while the development and implementation phase resulted in a responsive and mobile-friendly website. Rigorous testing and feedback helped fine-tune the final product. As a result, the new website not only enhances customer engagement but also provides a cohesive online presence that consolidates all essential information, driving increased traffic and customer satisfaction.
