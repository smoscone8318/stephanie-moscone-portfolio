Koko's Sushi Website Redesign

OVERVIEW

Koko Japanese Restaurant, a beloved establishment renowned for its authentic Japanese cuisine in Vancouver, is embarking on a digital transformation journey. Despite its exceptional culinary offerings, the current website fails to reflect the restaurant's true essence and fails to engage its online audience effectively.

The primary objective of this project is to revitalize Koko's online presence with a fresh, modern website design that highlights its exquisite sushi creations and traditional Japanese dishes. By focusing on improving menu presentation, providing up-to-date information on operating hours and location, and implementing seamless reservation functionality, the new website aims to captivate both loyal patrons and new customers alike, enticing them to experience the culinary delights of Koko Japanese Restaurant firsthand.

ROLE

Product Designer

User Research, Interaction, Visual design, Prototyping & Testing

March 2024 - April 2024

GOALS

The main goal of redesigning Koko's Sushi Restaurant website is to create a modern and appealing online experience. This includes using vibrant images of sushi dishes and clear menu options to showcase the variety and quality of their offerings. The website will also have improved navigation for easier access to important information like operating hours and location details. Overall, the aim is to make the website more engaging and user-friendly, encouraging customers to visit the restaurant and try their sushi.

Another key objective is to present Koko Japanese Restaurant in the best possible way to attract more customers. The website will highlight the freshness, quality, and variety of their sushi dishes. By showcasing the restaurant's offerings effectively, the goal is to inspire customers to visit and enjoy the culinary experience firsthand. The website redesign aims to turn online interest into actual visits, helping the restaurant grow and become more well-known in the community.

DESIGN

The design process for the Koko's Sushi Restaurant website redesign involved several key steps to ensure a modern and user-friendly outcome..

1. Research and Discovery:

The process began with research into the restaurant's brand, target audience, and competitors. This helped identify key design elements and features that would resonate with the target audience.

2. Wireframing and Prototyping:

Using the insights from the research phase, wireframes and prototypes were created to visualize the layout and functionality of the website. This step allowed for early feedback and iteration before moving on to the visual design.

3. Visual Design:

The visual design phase focused on creating a modern and inviting aesthetic that aligned with the restaurant's brand. This involved selecting color schemes, typography, and imagery that would enhance the user experience and showcase the restaurant's offerings effectively.

4. Content Creation:

High-quality images of sushi dishes and other menu items were selected to feature prominently on the website. Clear and concise menu options were also created to highlight the variety and quality of Koko's offerings.

5. Development and Implementation:

The website was developed using the chosen design elements and features. This included implementing improved navigation for easier access to important information and a streamlined reservation system to simplify the booking process.

6. Testiing and Feedback:

Throughout the design and development process, the website was tested to ensure it was functioning correctly and meeting user expectations. Feedback from users was also collected to make any necessary adjustments.

7. Launch and Optimization:

Once the website was finalized, it was launched to the public. Ongoing optimization and maintenance are essential to ensure the website continues to meet the needs of users and remains an effective tool for promoting Koko's Sushi Restaurant.

RESULTS

The redesign of Koko's Sushi Restaurant website has resulted in a more modern and user-friendly online experience. The vibrant images of sushi dishes and clear menu options have effectively showcased the variety and quality of the restaurant's offerings, enticing customers to explore further. The improved navigation has made it easier for users to find essential information, such as operating hours and location details, enhancing their overall browsing experience. The streamlined reservation system has also simplified the booking process, encouraging more customers to make reservations online. Overall, the redesign has successfully showcased Koko Japanese Restaurant in its best light, attracting more customers and enhancing its reputation in the local community.

Reflecting on the design process, it is evident that thorough research and planning were crucial in achieving the desired results. The iterative approach to design, including wireframing, prototyping, and testing, allowed for continuous improvement and ensured that the final product met the needs of both the restaurant and its customers. Collaboration between designers, developers, and stakeholders was key to the success of the project, ensuring that all aspects of the website aligned with the restaurant's brand and goals. Moving forward, ongoing optimization and maintenance will be essential to ensure that the website continues to effectively showcase Koko's Sushi Restaurant and attract new customers.
